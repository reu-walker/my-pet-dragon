My Pet Dragon

Dragon's Make Great Pets

When I was first looking for a pet I didn't feel like a dog or cat was right for me. Instead, while roaming around Norway in search of the perfect companion, I stumbled upon what I thought was a giant peacock egg. I was very excited about this discovery. Upon returning home I realized that:

  • It was not a peacock egg.
  • It growled.
  • It was warm to the touch
When it finally did hatch I was disappointed to discover that it wasn't a peacock but was elated to find that it was actually a dragon instead!
